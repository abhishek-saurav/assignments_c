My Fitness Routine Cards

A weekly workout plan

About This Plan

This fitness routine is for beginners. Each card shows one part of the workout.

🔥 Warm-Up

⏱ 10 minutes
  • Jumping jacks
  • Arm circles
  • Leg swings
  • Hip rotations
  • Light jog in place

🏃 Cardio

⏱ 20 minutes
  • High knees — 1 min
  • Burpees — 30 sec
  • Skip rope — 2 min
  • Mountain climbers
  • Rest — 1 min

💪 Strength

⏱ 20 minutes
  • Push-ups — 3×10
  • Squats — 3×15
  • Lunges — 3×10
  • Plank — 3×30 sec
  • Crunches — 3×20

Weekly Goal

Complete this routine 3 times a week — Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. Drink plenty of water, eat well, and rest on alternate days.
