Why I Like This Character

Tom is incredibly expressive and funny. Even though he almost never catches Jerry, he never gives up. His reactions and dramatic expressions make every episode entertaining.

Fun Facts

First appeared in 1940 in the short film "Puss Gets the Boot".

Tom has won seven Academy Awards alongside Jerry.

His full name is Thomas Jasper Cat Sr.
